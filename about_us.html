Our Story and Goal

Our story beings at a party with some close friends over a game of beer pongs. We were a struggling bunch of alcoholics, so ee tried to distract ourselves by playing some games. In hindsight we probably shouldn't have been playing beer pong, but anyways we couldn't use any drinks so we made our own. By combing different drinks, and making references to our favourite alcoholic drinks we ended up making something grand. From there we decided we could make a something out of this. So after some careful planning, and thinking we went out to get work. Took some time, but we were able pull our money together, and bought ourselves an old rundown bar. It took a lot of work to fix that bar up, but we enjoy it none the less. After long days of work we were finally ready to open up this bar. We even made time to come up with logo, and well the rest is history. We hope that we can bring you the joys we had with our drinks, and that you like them as much as we do!
